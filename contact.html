Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find your answer here? Use the form above to reach us directly.

Is TaskNova free to use? +
Yes — TaskNova is completely free. It was built as a university group project and is open to everyone. We may add premium features in the future, but the core experience will always remain free.
Do I need an account to use TaskNova? +
You need to sign in to access the Task Manager (to save your data securely). However, the home page, about page, and contact page are fully accessible without an account.
How does the Nova AI assistant work? +
Nova is powered by Google's Gemini API combined with the browser's Web Speech API for voice recognition. It can answer questions about TaskNova, help you manage your tasks, and have natural conversations — all right inside your browser.
Can I contribute to the project? +
Absolutely! TaskNova is a student project and we welcome feedback, ideas, and contributions. Reach out through the contact form above or find us on GitHub.
Is my data safe with TaskNova? +
We use Supabase for secure authentication and data storage. Your tasks and personal data are private to your account and are never shared or sold to third parties.
